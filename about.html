JoinUs

How Can I Join?

Meetings are right after school on Mondays and Fridays in room 615. Stop by for a meeting, join our facebook group, or just say hi to an officer if you see them in the hallway! Seniors in the club are generous to drive people home if they need a ride.

Why Should I Join?

Ridge Productions is a supportive community. No matter what you are interested in, you can make it happen here. Make great friends and make great things. Being creative and having fun have never mixed so well.

But I'm not artistic?!

This is NOT a club just for artists! Anyone who wants to be creative or just have a good time is welcome! Ridge Pro is vibrant from our members' wide spectrum of interests: from design to architecture to web development! Anyone is welcome at Ridge Pro!
